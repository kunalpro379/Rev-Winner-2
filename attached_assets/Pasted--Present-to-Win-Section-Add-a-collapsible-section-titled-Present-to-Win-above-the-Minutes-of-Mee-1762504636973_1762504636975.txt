“Present to Win” Section

Add a collapsible section titled “Present to Win” above the Minutes of Meeting section on the main Assistant page where the system analyzes conversations between the customer and the sales representative.

User Options (Selection Menu)

The section will display three options for the user to choose from:

1. Pitch Deck


2. Case Study


3. Battle Card




---

1. Pitch Deck

When the user selects Pitch Deck, generate a 5-slide mini deck that the user can slide left or right.

The deck should contain short, powerful text that directly aligns with the conversation context, helping the rep make a persuasive pitch.

The content should:

Reflect the solution the customer is looking for.

Endorse the product or service from the user’s selected Domain Expertise.

Be auto-generated instantly based on the conversation insights.


Purpose: Help the rep confidently demonstrate a contextual, relevant pitch to close the deal.



---

2. Case Study

When the user selects Case Study, generate a 2-slide visual summary automatically.

Each case study should include:

Problem/Challenge: A clear pain point identified in the conversation.

Solution: A detailed explanation showing how the proposed service or product addresses it.

Outcome: A measurable, positive result (e.g., improved ROI, faster deployment, reduced cost).


Goal: Provide a credible, contextual case study the rep can present instantly during or after the meeting.



---

3. Battle Card

When the user selects Battle Card, auto-generate a comparison table (maximum 2 slides).

The table should:

Compare the selected Domain Expertise solution/service with up to two nearest competitors.

Highlight key differentiators and competitive advantages.

If the customer mentions a specific competitor, ensure that competitor appears in the comparison and is clearly outperformed.


Goal: Help the rep handle objections effectively and position the product as the superior choice.



---

Performance & Experience

All three content types should be generated automatically, fast, and contextually relevant using conversation analytics.

Keep design minimal and easy to navigate, with an expand/collapse toggle for “Present to Win”.
