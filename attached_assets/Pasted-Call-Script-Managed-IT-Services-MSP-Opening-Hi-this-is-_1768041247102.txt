Opening
“Hi, this is Sandeep from Swift-Tech. We help businesses reduce IT risk, downtime, and surprise costs. I just need 2–3 minutes to understand if this is even relevant for you.”
Discovery Questions
Who manages your IT today—internal team or an MSP?
Have you faced any downtime, security incidents, or data loss in the last six months?
Is your IT spend predictable, or do you get surprise bills?
When was your backup and disaster recovery last tested?
If a ransomware attack happened tomorrow, how fast could you recover?
Does IT enable your growth—or slow it down?
